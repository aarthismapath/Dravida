Brief note on Dravida:
Dravida is a celebration of Indian & South Asian food beyond borders. Inspired by the
movement of people, spices, and stories, our menu explores how migration has shaped flavors
across the world. The food is deeply rooted yet ever-evolving—flavor-packed dishes that feel
nostalgic, comforting, and sometimes unexpected.

About us:
Dravida is a deeply personal restaurant by Chef Aarthi Sampath, exploring the evolution of
South Asian flavors across cultures and generations.
Every detail is intentional — from coconut shell chandeliers to hand-painted spice murals,
personal photographs, and music — each element thoughtfully chosen to create a sense of
place.
This is our home.
An experience of warm hospitality and rich, layered flavors that linger long after the meal.
