Our Story

About Dravida

Dravida is a deeply personal restaurant by Chef Aarthi Sampath, exploring the evolution of South Asian flavors across cultures and generations.

Every detail is intentional—from coconut shell chandeliers to hand-painted spice murals, personal photographs, and music—each element thoughtfully chosen to create a sense of place.

This is our home.

An experience of warm hospitality and rich, layered flavors that linger long after the meal.

Common Questions

FAQs

Can you accommodate dietary restrictions and allergies?

Yes. Please inform your server of any dietary restrictions or allergies, and we will do our best to accommodate your needs.

Is Dravida wheelchair accessible?

Dravida is wheelchair accessible; however, the bathrooms are located in the basement.

How do I make a reservation?

We accept reservations up to 14 days in advance on Resy.

Can I host a private event at Dravida?

Yes! We offer private dining and full restaurant buyouts. Contact us at aarthi@dravidanyc.com for details.

What are your hours?

We are open seven days a week, 5:00 PM to 11:00 PM.

Can I bring my own wine?

Please contact us at info@dravidanyc.com for details on our BYOB policy.

Do you offer takeout or delivery?

Please contact us at info@dravidanyc.com for the latest on takeout and delivery options.

Do you sell gift cards?

Please reach out to info@dravidanyc.com for information about gift cards.
