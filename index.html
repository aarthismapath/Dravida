Our Story

About Dravida

Dravida is a deeply personal restaurant by Chef Aarthi Sampath, exploring the evolution of South Asian flavors across cultures and generations.

Every detail is intentional—from coconut shell chandeliers to hand-painted spice murals, personal photographs, and music—each element thoughtfully chosen to create a sense of place.

This is our home.

An experience of warm hospitality and rich, layered flavors that linger long after the meal.

The Experience

Menu

A celebration of Indian & South Asian food beyond borders. Our menu changes with the seasons, highlighting the best ingredients and the stories behind them.

Small Plates

Rotating dishes inspired by South Asian street food & home cooking traditions—bold flavors, generous spice, meant to be shared.

Large Plates

Chef’s selection of entrees—layered, slow-built flavors rooted in tradition with modern technique. Each dish tells a story.

Desserts

Seasonal creations that honor South Asian confectionery heritage—cardamom, jaggery, saffron, and rose woven into every course.

Beverages

Curated cocktails, wines & non-alcoholic pairings—crafted to complement the bold, aromatic character of our cuisine.

Our menu evolves regularly. Please inform your server of any dietary restrictions or allergies.

Common Questions

FAQs

Everything you need to know before your visit.

Can you accommodate dietary restrictions and allergies?

Yes. Please inform your server of any dietary restrictions or allergies, and we will do our best to accommodate your needs.

Is Dravida wheelchair accessible?

Dravida is wheelchair accessible; however, the bathrooms are located in the basement.

How do I make a reservation?

We accept reservations up to 14 days in advance on Resy.

Can I host a private event at Dravida?

Yes! We offer private dining and full restaurant buyouts. Contact us at info@dravidanyc.com for details.

What are your hours?

We are open seven days a week, 4:30 PM to 10:30 PM.

Can I bring my own wine?

Yes. Our corkage fee is $50 per 750ml, with a maximum of one bottle per person. Please note, we do not allow corkage for wines that appear on our list.

Do you offer takeout or delivery?

At the moment, we don’t offer takeout or delivery.

Do you sell gift cards?

Yes! You can purchase a Dravida gift card directly online.
